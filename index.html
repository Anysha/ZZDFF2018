ABOUT


The ZIPZAPDEALS FOOD FEST is the biggest and most vibrant food festival in Delhi NCR. Since our inception we have engaged an estimated 1,50,000 patrons, with over 250 exhibitors offering an unforgettable culinary experience of indulging in an array of handpicked cuisines, entertainment and pop-up restaurants. With a new concept every show The ZIPZAPDEALS FOOD FEST is a foodie’s paradise not to be missed over the weekend.


ZipZapDeals Internet Private Limited is a student centric platform which empowers students & brands. Established in 2016, ZipZapDeals Internet Private Limited is India’s first and only college student-centric online platform that facilitates a GPS like tool to navigate their lifestyle requirement and needs. This innovative platform brings vendors (small vendors & merchant establishments) and big brands under common platform providing discovery of exclusive deals for the students. Zipzap facilitates real time updates on deals and offers, DIY for Vendors, daily statistic and more.
